Number of Pet Households across the United States

States in the South region have highest region average of pet households while the states in the West region has a pet households per 100.

Interestingly, most of the states in the West region has a below average number of pet households but California records the highest number of pet households in the country.


Which Pet has more Owners

While most states have a pretty balanced pets households, we can see that South states tends have significantly more Dog owners compared to Cat owners.

Dogs Win?

It seems like there is more Dog owners compared to Cat owners. Does this really mean that Dogs are more popular? Can we really conclude that American Households prefer Dogs?

Not Quite Right

Let's have a look at the raw popuation of Cats against Dogs.

Cats Win in Raw Population

Even though there might be slightly more Dog households, but in terms of raw population, cats outnumber dogs to the tune of 2 million.

How?

One simple explanation is that Cats are more compact. You can fit more Cats in a house than you can, say, golden retrievers.
