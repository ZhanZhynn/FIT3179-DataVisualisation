Pets Households by Region


Prominence of Reporting by Region

We can see how regional areas (Western and Eastern) have lower than average reporting while metropolitan areas (Northwest and Southern) have double the state average. Melbourne has both the highest rate of reporting and highest average number of reports at almost 14,000 while the state average sits at 3,000.

Gender and Age in Reporters

Women generally report less than men at all age groups however when we partition by offence type, women are more likely to report crime against their person while men report more property and deception cases.
